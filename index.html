Image Requirements
Format: JPG, JPEG, PNG; Size: between 48x48 and 4096x4096 pixels; File size: no larger than 2MB; Minimal size of face: the bounding box of a detected face is a square. The minimal side length of a square should be no less than 1/48 of the short side of image, and no less than 48 pixels.
